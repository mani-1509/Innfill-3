## 6. Payments and Fees

### 6.1 Payment Processing

**Payment Gateway:**
- We use **Razorpay** as our third-party payment processor
- All payments are processed in **Indian Rupees (INR)**
- Accepted payment methods: Credit/Debit cards, UPI, Net Banking, Wallets

**Payment Terms:**
- Payment is captured when client places an order
- Funds are held in escrow until order completion
- Payment is released to freelancer after successful delivery and client approval

### 6.2 Platform Fees

**Commission Structure:**
- Innfill charges a **14% commission** on all completed transactions
- Commission is calculated on the service price (before GST)
- Commission is deducted before payment release to freelancer

**GST (Goods and Services Tax):**
- **18% GST** is applied on the 14% commission
- Effective GST on transaction: 2.52% (18% of 14%)
- GST is paid to the Government of India as per tax regulations
- GST invoices available upon request

**Example Calculation:**
```
Service Price:           ₹10,000.00
Platform Commission:     ₹1,400.00 (14%)
GST on Commission:       ₹252.00 (18% of ₹1,400)
Client Pays:             ₹10,252.00
Freelancer Receives:     ₹8,600.00 (₹10,000 - ₹1,400)
Platform Receives:       ₹1,400.00
Government Receives:     ₹252.00
```

### 6.3 Payment Schedule

**For Freelancers:**

### 7.2 Refund Eligibility

**Eligible for Refund:**
- Service not delivered as described
- Freelancer fails to deliver within agreed timeline
- Freelancer becomes unresponsive after order acceptance
- Mutual agreement to cancel order
- Service violates platform policies or laws

**Not Eligible for Refund:**
- Client changes mind after delivery
- Client dissatisfaction due to unclear requirements
- Client wants different service than originally ordered
- Minor differences that don't affect overall quality
- Delays caused by client's failure to provide information

### 7.3 Refund Calculation

When a refund is issued, the following deductions apply:

**Processing Fee:** 4% of service price  
**GST:** Non-refundable (₹252 in above example)

**Refund Formula:**
```
Refund Amount = Service Price - 4% Processing Fee
```

**Example:**
```
Client Paid:              ₹10,252.00
Service Price:            ₹10,000.00
GST (non-refundable):     ₹252.00
Processing Fee (4%):      ₹400.00
Refund to Client:         ₹9,600.00
Client Loss:              ₹652.00 (4% + GST)
```

**Refund Timeline:**
- Refunds initiated immediately upon approval
- Processing time: 5-7 business days
- Refunds credited to original payment method
- Razorpay handles refund processing

**Partial Refunds:**
- May be issued for partially completed work
- Amount determined based on work completed
- Requires mutual agreement or dispute resolution

### 7.4 Refund Process

1. **Request Refund:** Client submits cancellation request with reason
2. **Review:** Platform reviews request and order details
3. **Freelancer Response:** Freelancer notified and can provide input
4. **Decision:** Refund approved or denied based on policy
5. **Processing:** Approved refunds processed within 24 hours
6. **Completion:** Refund credited within 5-7 business days

### 7.5 Dispute Resolution

If refund request is disputed:
